Simplicity in IP Networks

Jeff Loughridge of Brooks Consulting removes the complexity from IP networks. In the design and operation of IP infrastructure, simplicity is critical in minimizing your CAPEX and OPEX. Jeff will collaborate with your company to tweak or overhaul your network design and processes.
